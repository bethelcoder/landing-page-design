FAQs

Find answers to common questions before reaching out to our support team.

How do I sign up?

To sign up, visit our registration page and fill out the required fields. You will receive a confirmation email to activate your account. Once activated, you can start managing your projects.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. This ensures flexibility for both freelancers and clients. Check our payment page for more details.

Can I edit my profile?

Yes, you can easily edit your profile at any time. Simply go to the profile settings section after logging in. Make your changes and save them to update your information.

How do I contact support?

You can contact our support team through the 'Contact Us' page. Fill out the form with your inquiry, and we will get back to you promptly. Alternatively, you can reach us via email.

Is there a mobile app?

Currently, we do not have a mobile app. However, our website is fully responsive and can be accessed on mobile devices. We are considering developing an app in the future.
